# Privacy Policy

Telemost Hotkeys does not collect, store, sell, or transmit any user data.

The extension runs only on Yandex Telemost pages and the embedded Yandex chat
iframe used by Telemost. It listens for predefined keyboard shortcuts locally in
the browser and clicks matching Telemost interface buttons.

No personal information, chat messages, meeting content, browsing history, or
user activity data is collected or sent to third parties.
